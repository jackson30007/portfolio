Mayhem Mania, later renamed to Mixed Arcade was a gamemode on the Project Mayhem network. It featured 27 unique fast-paced minigames. Each game would last around 3-5 minutes. Players would earn XP and level up based on their performance. Players could unlock kits they could use for each minigame, giving them small advantage.

After Project Mayhem closed, this gamemode was re-released as a standalone server called Speedy Games. New minigames were added & tons of cosmetics were added, including emotes, victory dances, & more.
