The Project Myahem Network had many unique gamemodes, and was able to foster a dedicated community. The network had 450k+ unique joins and a peak of over 300 concurrent players. My role was the lead developer in a very small team. I was the only consistent developer and had to do the majority of dev work. Working on Project Mayhem was my first big project and due to the small team, I had to learn a ton while on the job.

  • Friend & Party systems
